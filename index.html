Send Availability Texts in as little as 2 touches!

The Fire Off app is a very efficient phone application designed to make managing your FSI availability as quick and simple as possible. It utilises the text messaging functionality provided by the Gartan FSI availability system. It is available for iPhone and Android devices.

Easy To Use

Download the app, add your FSI pin code and text messaging number and you are ready to go!.

Very Fast

Fire Off has been designed to be very easy and quick - book off for an hour with only two touches, once the app is opened.

No Wifi Required

Since Fire Off uses the phone network, you can book off quickly and easily without wifi available.

Delete Bookings

Bookings can also be deleted using Fire Off.

Messages Editable

Messages can easily be edited before sending to change dates to far in the future.

Sanity Check Before Sending

Messages are translated into "Human" for sanity checking before sending, eg "Booking off from 0800 today until 1200 tomorrow.".
